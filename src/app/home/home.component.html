Why Quizly

Measure

You can use as a means to test your understanding of a topic you've just read, a class you've just attended, or just assess your knowledge of a topic at your own time and venue.

As real as possible

The mock assessment is desgined to mimick real world assesment scenatios as much as possible including inability to copy and paste, inabilty to leave your test screen without consequences, submission immediately the timer is up

High number of questions

Each topic up for assesment has a high ratio of available question to assessment question. There is a high number of questions available for assesment per each topic

Curriculum based questions

The questions are based on the curent curriculum acreddited by the NUC for the department under which the title exists.

Ease of Access

All you need is your Mobile phone, or your Personal computer (PC) with a working internet and you are ready to go. You can use at any time of the day from anywhere you can make use of your mobile and internet
